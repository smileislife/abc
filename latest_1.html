Her words create a world of light, hope and endless possibility.
I feel so lucky to have you as part of my life now and always in my heart.
You will never really know how special you are to me.

With you, everything seems so beautiful. I have found a new me who's more calm and caring.

I don’t know what she was doing but year after year, she was looking more and more beautiful.

I swear, it’s like witchcraft! And I’m not just talking about the way she look,
she become more and more beautiful within as the years pass too.
There are things that are irreplaceable, and so are she was to me.

When I look at her, I see one who is cute in a million ways that words can suffice to say.
I’m glad to have her and proud to celebrate her birthday today.

I pray your days are spent happily. Happy birthday to you, my lucky charm.
